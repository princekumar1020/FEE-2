Book Title : The Psychology of Money

Author : Morgan Housel

Reviews :

  • Rating :     4.7/5    ★★★★☆

    • Pros :
    • Encourages self-awareness
    • Teaches behavioral skills

    Cons:
    • Not a traditional finance book
    • Not a get-rich-quick book
Summary :

"The Psychology of Money" by Morgan Housel: How our minds play tricks with money, and how to outsmart them.

Recommendation:

"If you enjoy classic literature and are looking for a thought-provoking read,
this book is a must-read."
